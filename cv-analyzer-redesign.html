FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HireIQ and how it can transform your hiring process.

What is HireIQ? +
CV Analyzer is an AI-powered platform that analyzes and ranks CVs based on how well they match a specific job description. It helps recruiters make faster and smarter hiring decisions by providing detailed compatibility scores and insights.
How does HireIQ work? +
Simply upload one or more CVs along with a job description. Our system uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) algorithms to evaluate each CV and provide compatibility scores, detailed skill matching analysis, gap identification, and actionable insights for improvement.
What kind of files can I upload? +
You can upload CVs in commonly used formats such as PDF, DOC, DOCX, or TXT. Our system supports multiple file formats to ensure maximum compatibility and ease of use.
Can I analyze multiple CVs at once? +
Absolutely! You can upload multiple CVs against a single job description to compare and rank candidates more efficiently. This feature is perfect for recruiters managing large candidate pools.
Do I need to sign up to use HireIQ? +
You can try our basic features without signing up. For full access to advanced features and to save your analysis history, we recommend creating a free account.
What insights do I get from the analysis? +
You'll receive comprehensive scoring across contact info, experience, education, skills, and formatting. Additionally, you'll get skill gap analysis, improvement suggestions, and detailed compatibility scores.
Is HireIQ free to use? +
We offer a free tier with basic features. For advanced analytics, bulk processing, and premium support, we offer affordable subscription plans tailored to your needs.
